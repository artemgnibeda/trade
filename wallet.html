HOW TO DO DEPOSIT WITH A CREDIT/DEBIT CARD OR OTHER ONLINE SERVICES.

Broker Cryptobo.com works only on Bitcoin, so to deposit funds from a credit/debit card or other services such as webmoney, QIWI, Yandex Money, etc., it is required to buy Bitcoin at the beginning. This is done very simply and quickly.

Please follow these steps:

1. Click the Deposit button at the top of the page.

2. You will be taken to the payment initiation menu. You need to enter the amount in the bitcoins you want to deposit. Сonversion rate from the US dollar you can see here - http://preev.com/btc/usd
